About 2magpiesseo

A small & agile digital agency helping North West England businesses succeed online since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, 2magpiesseo began as a passion project to help small businesses in the North West of England compete in the digital landscape. What started as a two-person operation has grown into a trusted partner for over 250 businesses across Greater Manchester, Cheshire, Merseyside, and Lancashire.

We believe that every business, regardless of size, deserves access to professional digital marketing services. Our mission is to level the playing field by providing enterprise-level SEO, digital marketing, and web development services at prices that work for small and medium businesses.

Unlike large agencies that treat clients as account numbers, we build genuine partnerships. We take the time to understand your business, your goals, and your challenges. This personal approach has resulted in long-term relationships with clients who see us as an extension of their team.

Our Values

Transparency

No jargon, no hidden costs, no surprises. We explain everything in plain English and provide regular, detailed reports on your campaign performance.

Results-Driven

We're not interested in vanity metrics. Every strategy we implement is designed to drive real business results - more leads, more sales, more revenue.

Personal Service

You'll work directly with experienced team members, not junior staff or account managers. We're here when you need us, responsive and accessible.
