Frequently Asked Questions

Is my money safe in a pre-paid plan?

Yes, absolutely. Your money is held in an independent trust fund, not by us. This means it's completely secure and protected. The trust is regulated and your money can only be released to carry out your funeral. If we were ever unable to provide your funeral, another funeral director would be appointed to carry out your wishes.

What if I move away from the area?

Your plan is fully transferable. If you move elsewhere in the UK, we can arrange for a funeral director in your new area to provide the funeral. If you move abroad, the plan value can be refunded. The plan belongs to you and moves with you.

Can I change my mind or cancel?

Yes. You have 30 days after purchase to cancel for a full refund. After this, you can still cancel but a cancellation fee applies. You can also change elements of your plan at any time - just contact us to discuss amendments. We want you to be completely happy with your arrangements.

Are all costs covered?

Our services and the services of third parties included in your plan are fully covered. This typically includes crematorium or cemetery fees at today's prices. Some items like flowers, memorials, or wake venues are usually excluded as these are often arranged by families at the time. We'll clearly explain what's included and what isn't.

What happens if I die before completing payments?

If you're paying by instalments and pass away after 12 months of payments, your funeral is fully covered with no more to pay. If it's within the first 12 months, your family can either pay the outstanding balance or receive a refund of payments made less an administration fee.

How do I know what plan is right for me?

We'll help you choose. During our consultation, we'll discuss your preferences and budget to find the right plan. There's no pressure and no obligation. Many people find it helpful to involve a family member in discussions. We can provide written information to take away and consider before deciding.
