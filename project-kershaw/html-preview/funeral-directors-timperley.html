How far are you from Timperley?

Do you collect from Wythenshawe Hospital?

Yes, we regularly collect from Wythenshawe Hospital and are familiar with their procedures. We can liaise with the bereavement office and handle all necessary paperwork. Collection can be arranged promptly at any time.

Can you arrange services at Altrincham Crematorium?

Yes, Altrincham Crematorium is our local crematorium and we arrange services there regularly. We know the staff well and can advise on service times, music facilities, and webcast options. We handle all the booking and coordination.

Do you serve all faiths in Timperley?

Absolutely. Timperley is a diverse community and we serve families of all faiths and none. We have experience with Christian, Muslim, Jewish, Hindu, and Sikh funerals, as well as humanist and civil ceremonies.

What are your costs for Timperley residents?

Our prices are the same regardless of where you live. Direct cremation starts from £995, with traditional funerals from around £3,495. We provide transparent, itemized quotes with no hidden charges. Payment plans are available.
