Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if someone dies at home?

First, call their GP or 111 if out of hours to confirm the death. Then call us on 0161 969 2288. We'll guide you through everything and can collect your loved one when you're ready. There's no rush - take the time you need. We're here to support you through each step.

Can you collect from hospitals and care homes?

Yes, we regularly collect from all local hospitals including Wythenshawe, Manchester Royal Infirmary, Salford Royal, and Stepping Hill. We also collect from care homes, hospices, and private residences throughout Greater Manchester. We handle all necessary paperwork with the facility.

How quickly can you respond?

We aim to collect your loved one within 2-4 hours of your call, any time of day or night. However, there's no pressure - if you need more time to say goodbye, we'll wait until you're ready. Some families prefer to wait until morning, which is perfectly fine.

Do you charge extra for out-of-hours calls?

No, never. Our 24/7 service is part of what we do. Whether you call us at 2pm on a Tuesday or 2am on Christmas Day, there are no additional charges for evening, weekend, or holiday collections. The price is the same.

Can I email instead of calling?

For immediate needs, please call us on 0161 969 2288 as emails aren't monitored 24/7. For general enquiries, pre-planning discussions, or to request information, email is fine. We typically respond to emails within one business day.
