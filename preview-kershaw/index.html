Over 130 Years of Caring Service

Arthur Kershaw Funeral Services has been supporting families throughout Sale, Stretford, Altrincham and surrounding areas for over 130 years.

As an independent, family-owned funeral directors, we understand that every family is unique and every farewell should be personal. Our experienced team provides compassionate guidance through every step of arranging a funeral, ensuring your loved one receives the dignified farewell they deserve.

From our funeral home on Washway Road in Sale, we offer a complete range of funeral services including traditional burials, cremations, direct cremations, and pre-paid funeral plans. Our modern facilities include private Chapels of Rest where you can spend time with your loved one in peaceful surroundings.

Plan Ahead with Pre-Paid Funeral Plans

Peace of Mind for You and Your Family

A pre-paid funeral plan allows you to arrange and pay for your funeral in advance, protecting your loved ones from difficult decisions and financial burden during an emotional time.

  • Fix funeral costs at today's prices
  • Specify your personal wishes
  • Reduce stress for your family
  • Flexible payment options available
