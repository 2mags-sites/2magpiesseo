What are Emergency Express Services?

Emergency express services are our rapid response solution for critical hydraulic failures that threaten to shut down your operations. At Dynasurf, we understand that equipment failure can cost thousands of pounds per hour in lost production, which is why we maintain dedicated emergency response capabilities with materials, skilled technicians, and priority scheduling available 24/7.

Our express service covers the full range of hydraulic repairs including same-day cylinder honing, urgent chrome plating, emergency seal replacement, and critical component manufacturing. We maintain extensive stocks of commonly needed materials and components, enabling us to respond immediately to most emergency situations.

With over 50 years of experience handling urgent repairs, our team understands the pressure of equipment failures and works systematically to diagnose problems, implement solutions, and restore your equipment to operation as quickly as possible while maintaining our exacting quality standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to an emergency hydraulic failure? ‚ñº

Our emergency line is staffed 24/7 with immediate response. We can typically arrange same-day collection throughout the UK and, depending on the repair required, often complete simple repairs within hours. Complex repairs may take longer but receive priority scheduling to minimise your downtime. We'll provide an accurate timeline immediately after assessment.

What additional costs are involved for emergency express service? ‚ñº

Emergency service includes priority charges for urgent collection, extended hours operation, and express delivery. These charges vary depending on the urgency and complexity of the repair. We provide transparent pricing upfront, and often the emergency charges are offset by the savings from reduced downtime. Many clients find the express service cost-effective compared to production losses.

Can you provide temporary solutions while permanent repairs are completed? ‚ñº

Yes, where possible we can provide temporary repair solutions to get your equipment operational while permanent repairs are completed. This might include emergency seal replacement, temporary chrome plating, or expedited component manufacturing. We'll assess each situation and recommend the best approach to minimise your operational disruption.

Do emergency repairs come with the same warranty as standard work? ‚ñº

Absolutely. All emergency repairs carry the same 12-month warranty as our standard work, covering workmanship and components replaced during the repair. We never compromise on quality standards, even under emergency conditions. The only difference is the accelerated timeline, not the quality of work performed.

What areas do you cover for emergency collection and delivery? ‚ñº

We provide emergency collection and delivery throughout the UK. Local to Cheshire and surrounding areas, we can often provide same-day service with our own vehicles. For more distant locations, we use emergency courier services to ensure rapid collection and delivery. Collection can usually be arranged within 4-6 hours of your call.

Can you provide on-site emergency assessment or repairs? ‚ñº

For critical situations, we can arrange on-site assessment with our mobile service. While most precision repairs require our workshop facilities, we can perform field diagnosis, temporary repairs, and component removal/installation on-site. This service is particularly valuable for large installations where component removal is complex or time-consuming.
