Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you provide a quote for my project? ‚ñº

We aim to provide quotes within 2 hours during business hours for standard enquiries. Complex projects may require site visit or detailed technical assessment, which we can usually arrange within 24 hours. For urgent requirements, call us directly for immediate pricing.

Do you offer collection and delivery services? ‚ñº

Yes, we provide comprehensive collection and delivery throughout the UK. Local collection (Cheshire and surrounding areas) is usually same-day, while nationwide service is typically next working day. Emergency collection can be arranged for urgent repairs.

What information do you need for an accurate quote? ‚ñº

For hydraulic repairs: component dimensions, operating pressure, and photos of damage. For manufacturing: technical drawings or sample parts. For all services: quantity, timeline, and specific requirements. The more detail you provide, the more accurate our quote will be.

Can I visit your workshop to discuss my requirements? ‚ñº

Absolutely. We welcome visitors to our Sandbach facility. Please call ahead to arrange an appointment so we can ensure the right technical person is available to discuss your specific requirements. Workshop tours are available to showcase our capabilities.

Do you work with international customers? ‚ñº

Yes, we serve customers throughout Europe and internationally. We can arrange international shipping and provide all necessary documentation for customs clearance. Our quality certifications are recognised internationally for regulated industries.

What payment terms do you offer? ‚ñº

We offer various payment terms depending on order value and customer relationship. Standard terms are 30 days for established accounts. We accept bank transfer, cheque, and card payments. Account terms available for regular trade customers with credit approval.
