50+ Years of Engineering Excellence

Established in 1971, Dynasurf has built a reputation as one of the UK's leading precision engineering companies. Our name, derived from "Dynamic Surfaces," reflects our commitment to delivering exceptional surface engineering solutions.

From our purpose-built facility in Sandbach, Cheshire, we serve industries across the UK with rapid turnaround times and uncompromising quality. Our investment in state-of-the-art equipment allows us to handle projects that others cannot, including cylinder honing up to 300mm diameter and 2.2 meters in length.

Whether you need emergency repairs or scheduled maintenance, our experienced team is ready to deliver solutions that keep your equipment running at peak performance.
