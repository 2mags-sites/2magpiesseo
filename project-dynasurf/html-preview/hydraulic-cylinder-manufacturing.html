What is Custom Hydraulic Cylinder Manufacturing?

Custom hydraulic cylinder manufacturing involves the complete design and production of bespoke hydraulic components tailored to specific application requirements. At Dynasurf, we specialise in manufacturing hydraulic cylinders, pistons, rods, gland caps, and sealing systems that meet unique operational demands where standard components simply won't suffice.

Our manufacturing capabilities extend from single prototypes to production runs, covering everything from heavy-duty industrial cylinders to precision pneumatic actuators. With over 50 years of engineering expertise, we understand that each application has unique pressure, stroke, mounting, and environmental requirements that demand custom solutions.

We maintain extensive material stocks including various grades of steel, stainless steel, bronze, and specialist alloys, enabling us to offer same-day manufacturing for urgent requirements. Our CNC machining centres and skilled craftsmen ensure every component meets or exceeds OEM specifications.

Frequently Asked Questions

What information do you need to manufacture a custom hydraulic cylinder? ‚ñº

We need the bore diameter, rod diameter, stroke length, operating pressure, mounting style, and environmental conditions. If you have existing drawings or a damaged cylinder for reference, this helps significantly. We can also reverse-engineer from samples or work from your application requirements to design the optimal solution.

How long does custom hydraulic cylinder manufacturing take? ‚ñº

Lead times vary depending on complexity and current workload. Simple cylinders with materials in stock can be manufactured within 3-5 working days. Complex assemblies or those requiring special materials typically take 2-3 weeks. We can expedite urgent orders and offer same-day service for emergency requirements where possible.

Do you provide design services for custom cylinders? ‚ñº

Yes, we offer complete design services including 3D CAD modelling, stress analysis, and material selection. Our experienced engineers work with you to optimise the design for your specific application, ensuring reliability, performance, and cost-effectiveness. We can also improve on existing designs for better longevity.

What materials can you work with for custom manufacturing? ‚ñº

We work with a wide range of materials including EN24, EN8, stainless steel 316/304, bronze, aluminium, and specialist alloys. For extreme environments, we can specify exotic materials such as duplex stainless steel, titanium, or super alloys. Surface treatments include hard chrome plating, nitriding, anodising, and various protective coatings.

Can you manufacture hydraulic cylinders for high-pressure applications? ‚ñº

Absolutely. We regularly manufacture cylinders for high-pressure applications up to 700 bar and beyond. This involves careful material selection, wall thickness calculations, appropriate sealing systems, and rigorous testing procedures. All high-pressure cylinders are pressure tested to 1.5 times working pressure before delivery.

Do you offer prototyping services for new designs? ‚ñº

Yes, we provide prototyping services for new cylinder designs. This allows you to test form, fit, and function before committing to production quantities. We can manufacture single prototypes or small batches for field testing, with the option to scale up to production volumes once the design is proven.
