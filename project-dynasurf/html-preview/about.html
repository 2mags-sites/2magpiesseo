Our Company Story

Established in 1971, Dynasurf UK Ltd has been at the forefront of precision engineering and surface treatment technology for over five decades. What began as a small engineering workshop in Sandbach, Cheshire, has evolved into one of the UK's leading specialists in hydraulic system repair, cylinder honing, hard chrome plating, and precision component manufacturing.

Throughout our journey, we've remained true to our founding principles of technical excellence, customer service, and continuous innovation. Our commitment to quality has seen us grow from serving local agricultural and construction businesses to supporting major industrial manufacturers, automotive companies, and engineering specialists across the United Kingdom and beyond.

Today, Dynasurf combines traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology, maintaining the personal service of a family business while delivering the capabilities and quality standards demanded by modern industry. Our reputation has been built component by component, repair by repair, through decades of consistent performance and reliability.
