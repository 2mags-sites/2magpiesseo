Understanding Traditional Funeral Services

Every funeral can be bespoke and tailored for every family we care for.

A traditional funeral service remains the most popular and recognizable option for families in Liverpool and Merseyside. At Barringtons, we believe that every life is unique, and every funeral should reflect the individual being remembered. Our traditional funeral service provides a framework that can be completely personalised to create a meaningful farewell.

This service is suitable for families who wish to have a formal ceremony, whether religious or non-religious, with the opportunity for friends and family to gather, pay their respects, and celebrate a life lived. We provide compassionate support throughout the entire process, ensuring every detail is handled with care and professionalism.

As family-owned independent funeral directors, we take time to understand your wishes and provide the personal attention that larger companies often cannot offer. Our membership with the National Association of Funeral Directors ensures we maintain the highest professional standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a traditional funeral cost?

Costs vary depending on your specific requirements and choices. We provide transparent, itemised quotes with no hidden charges. Our standard price list is available at all our offices in Waterloo, Formby, and Netherton. We offer options to suit different budgets and can discuss payment options if needed.

Can we visit our loved one before the funeral?

Yes, we have chapels of rest at our premises where you can spend time with your loved one. Visits can be arranged by appointment at a time that suits you. We ensure your loved one is presented with dignity and respect.

Do you offer eco-friendly funeral options?

Absolutely. We're members of the Association of Green Funeral Directors and offer zero-emission electric vehicles. We also plant a tree for every funeral we conduct - over 3,000 trees to date. Eco-friendly coffins and natural burial options are available.

Can you accommodate religious or cultural requirements?

Yes, we have experience arranging funerals for all faiths and cultures. We work closely with religious leaders and communities across Liverpool and Merseyside to ensure all requirements are met with respect and understanding.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Liverpool, Waterloo, Formby, Bootle, Netherton, Seaforth, and surrounding areas of Merseyside. With three convenient locations, we're always nearby when you need us. We can also arrange repatriation services if required.

How soon after death should we contact you?

You can contact us immediately, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. There's no rush - take the time you need. We're here whenever you're ready to begin making arrangements. Early contact allows us to provide guidance on immediate steps and bring your loved one into our care when appropriate.
