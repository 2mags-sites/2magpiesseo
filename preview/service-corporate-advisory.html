Frequently Asked Questions

When should we engage corporate advisory services?

Early engagement is crucial for successful transactions. Ideally, involve advisors during strategic planning, before approaching targets or buyers. This allows proper structuring, due diligence preparation, and negotiation strategy development.

What's the typical M&A transaction timeline?

Simple transactions may complete in 2-3 months, while complex cross-border deals typically take 6-12 months. Timeline depends on due diligence complexity, regulatory approvals, financing arrangements, and negotiation dynamics.

How do you handle cross-border transactions?

We coordinate with our network of international law firms to provide seamless multi-jurisdictional advice. Our team manages the overall transaction while local counsel handles jurisdiction-specific requirements.

What due diligence is typically required?

Standard due diligence covers legal, financial, tax, commercial, and operational aspects. We coordinate specialist advisors and manage data rooms, focusing on material risks and value drivers specific to your transaction.
