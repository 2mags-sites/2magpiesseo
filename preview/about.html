About Robert Lee Law Offices

Three decades of legal excellence serving Hong Kong and international clients with integrity, expertise, and innovative solutions.

Our History

Established in 1994, Robert Lee Law Offices has grown from a boutique practice to one of Hong Kong's most respected international law firms, serving clients across Asia-Pacific and beyond.

1994

Foundation & Early Years

Robert Lee Law Offices established in Hong Kong, focusing on corporate law and cross-border transactions. Initial practice concentrated on serving international businesses entering the Hong Kong and Chinese markets.

2001

Expansion & Specialization

Expanded practice areas to include dispute resolution, intellectual property, and immigration law. Began serving multinational corporations with complex regional legal requirements.

2008

InterAsia Alliance Formation

Co-founded the InterAsia Legal Alliance, creating strategic partnerships across Asia-Pacific. This milestone enabled seamless cross-border legal services for international clients.

2015

Wealth Management Focus

Launched dedicated wealth management and family office practice, responding to growing demand for sophisticated estate planning and trust services in Hong Kong.

2020

Digital Innovation

Implemented cutting-edge legal technology and digital service delivery, ensuring continued excellence during global challenges while maintaining personal client relationships.

2024

30 Years of Excellence

Celebrating three decades of legal excellence with over 5,000 clients served, maintaining our position as a leading international law firm in Hong Kong.
