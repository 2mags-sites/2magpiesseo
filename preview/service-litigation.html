Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I receive a legal claim?

Contact us immediately. Time limits for responding to legal claims are strict. We'll review the claim, advise on merits, and prepare your defense strategy. Early action often leads to better outcomes and more options for resolution.

How long does litigation typically take in Hong Kong?

Simple cases may resolve in 6-12 months, while complex commercial litigation can take 2-3 years to reach trial. Factors include court schedules, case complexity, and settlement negotiations. We work to expedite resolution wherever possible.

What are the costs involved in litigation?

Costs vary based on complexity and duration. We provide transparent fee estimates and discuss funding options including conditional fee arrangements, after-the-event insurance, and third-party funding where appropriate.

Should I choose litigation or arbitration?

Each has advantages. Litigation offers appeal rights and established procedures. Arbitration provides confidentiality, expert arbitrators, and international enforceability. We'll advise based on your specific circumstances and objectives.

Can Hong Kong judgments be enforced in mainland China?

Yes, under the 2019 reciprocal enforcement arrangement, many Hong Kong judgments can be enforced in mainland China. We have extensive experience navigating cross-border enforcement procedures.
