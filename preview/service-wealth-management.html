Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start estate planning?

Estate planning should begin when you have significant assets or family responsibilities. Early planning provides more options and can result in substantial tax savings. For business owners and high net worth individuals, planning becomes critical as wealth grows.

What are the benefits of establishing a family trust?

Family trusts provide asset protection, tax efficiency, succession planning, and privacy. They can protect assets from creditors, reduce tax burdens, ensure smooth wealth transfer, and maintain confidentiality of family financial affairs.

How do you handle cross-border tax issues?

We analyze tax implications across all relevant jurisdictions, utilize double taxation treaties, and structure arrangements to minimize overall tax burden while ensuring compliance with all reporting requirements including CRS and FATCA.

What is involved in family office setup?

Family office setup involves establishing governance structures, investment policies, administrative systems, and next-generation programs. We handle legal structure, regulatory compliance, and coordinate with service providers for comprehensive family wealth management.

How do you ensure privacy and confidentiality?

We maintain strict confidentiality through lawyer-client privilege, secure document handling, need-to-know information sharing, and anonymous structure design where appropriate. All staff sign comprehensive confidentiality agreements.
