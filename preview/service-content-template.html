Frequently Asked Questions

How long does company formation take?

Standard incorporation takes 1-2 weeks. Express services available for 1-3 business days.

What are the minimum requirements?

One shareholder, one director (can be same person), Hong Kong company secretary, registered office address, and HKD 1 minimum capital.

Can foreigners own 100% of a Hong Kong company?

Yes, Hong Kong allows 100% foreign ownership with no restrictions on foreign shareholders or directors.
