Frequently Asked Questions

What are the basic requirements for a Hong Kong work visa?

Key requirements include a confirmed job offer, relevant qualifications and experience, clean criminal record, and ability to support yourself financially. The position must meet salary benchmarks and demonstrate that no suitable local candidate is available.

How long does the work visa application take?

Standard processing takes 4-6 weeks for straightforward applications. Complex cases may take 8-12 weeks. We offer expedited processing services for urgent cases, which can reduce timelines significantly.

Can my family accompany me to Hong Kong?

Yes, spouses and unmarried dependent children under 18 can apply for dependent visas. Spouses can also apply for permission to work in Hong Kong. We handle all family visa applications simultaneously.

What is the minimum salary requirement?

There's no fixed minimum salary, but compensation should be competitive for the role and industry. Immigration Department considers market rates, qualifications, and experience. Generally, professional roles start around HK$40,000+ monthly.

Can I change employers on a work visa?

Yes, but you must notify Immigration Department before changing employers. We assist with change of employer applications, ensuring compliance with visa conditions and smooth transition to new employment.

What are the pathways to permanent residency?

After 7 years of continuous ordinary residence in Hong Kong, you may apply for Right of Abode (permanent residency). We provide comprehensive planning and assistance throughout this process.
