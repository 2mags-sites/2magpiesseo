Types of Patents

Different patent categories for various inventions

Utility Patents

Protection for new and useful processes, machines, compositions of matter, or improvements thereof.

  • 20-year protection term
  • Most common patent type
  • Covers functional aspects

Design Patents

Protection for new, original, and ornamental designs of manufactured articles.

  • 15-year protection term
  • Visual appearance focus
  • Ornamental features
