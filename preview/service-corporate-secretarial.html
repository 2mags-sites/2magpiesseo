Key Compliance Deadlines

Stay on top of important filing deadlines with our compliance calendar:

Annual Return Filing

Due within 42 days of the company's incorporation anniversary date.

Penalty: HK$870 if filed late
Significant Controller Register

Must be maintained and updated within 7 days of any changes.

Penalty: Up to HK$25,000 for non-compliance
Business Registration Renewal

Annual renewal required with payment of business registration fee.

Fee: HK$2,250 (or HK$3,950 if filed late)
Change Notifications

Director, secretary, or registered office changes must be filed within 15 days.

Penalty: HK$300 per director per day if late

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I miss the annual return filing deadline?

Late filing incurs a penalty of HK$870. If not filed within 3 months, the company may be struck off the register. We provide early warning systems to prevent missed deadlines and can assist with late filings if necessary.

Can you serve as company secretary for listed companies?

Yes, we provide qualified company secretary services for listed companies, ensuring compliance with Listing Rules and all statutory requirements. Our team includes qualified company secretaries with extensive public company experience.

What is included in your registered office service?

Our registered office service includes a Hong Kong business address for your company, mail handling and forwarding, document receipt, and government correspondence management. We also provide meeting room facilities when needed.

How do you ensure data security and confidentiality?

We maintain strict confidentiality protocols with secure document storage, encrypted data transmission, and restricted access controls. Our systems are regularly audited and comply with international data protection standards.

Can you handle share transfers and capital changes?

Yes, we handle all types of share transfers, capital restructuring, and corporate changes. This includes preparing transfer documents, updating registers, filing with Companies Registry, and ensuring stamp duty compliance.

What are the benefits of outsourcing corporate secretarial functions?

Outsourcing provides cost savings, access to expertise, reduced compliance risk, and allows management to focus on core business. You also benefit from our systems, processes, and continuous regulatory updates without internal investment.
