Trademark Classes

Understanding the Nice Classification system

Goods Classes (1-34)
  • Chemicals, pharmaceuticals
  • Clothing, footwear
  • Electronics, machinery
  • Food and beverages
  • Toys, sporting goods
Services Classes (35-45)
  • Business services, advertising
  • Financial services, insurance
  • Technology, communication
  • Education, entertainment
  • Legal services, medical
Important: Selecting the correct trademark classes is crucial for effective protection. Our experts will help you choose the optimal classes for your business.
