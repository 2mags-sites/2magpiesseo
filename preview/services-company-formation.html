Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to incorporate a company in Hong Kong?

The standard incorporation process typically takes 1-2 weeks from submission of all required documents. Express incorporation services are available for urgent requirements, which can be completed within 1-3 business days.

What are the minimum requirements for Hong Kong company formation?

You need at least one shareholder and one director (can be the same person), a company secretary (must be Hong Kong resident or company), a registered office address in Hong Kong, and minimum share capital of HKD 1.

Can foreigners own 100% of a Hong Kong company?

Yes, Hong Kong allows 100% foreign ownership of companies. There are no restrictions on foreign shareholders or directors, making it an attractive jurisdiction for international businesses.

What ongoing compliance requirements are there?

Hong Kong companies must file annual returns, maintain proper books of accounts, conduct annual audits (unless exempted), renew business registration annually, and hold annual general meetings.
