Making Every Funeral Unique

Your loved one was unique - their farewell should be too

At Barringtons, we believe that every person has a story worth telling and every life deserves to be celebrated in its own special way. Our personalisation services help you create a funeral that truly reflects who your loved one was, what they loved, and the impact they had on those around them.

Musical Tributes

Whether it's their favourite song, a piece they played, or music that holds special meaning, we can incorporate musical elements that celebrate their passion and bring comfort to those grieving.

  • Live musicians and vocalists
  • Recorded music of their choice
  • Musical instruments they loved
  • Songs that tell their story

Visual Memories

Create a visual celebration of their life through photographs, videos, and personal mementos that showcase the moments and achievements that defined them.

  • Memory boards with life photos
  • Video tributes and slideshows
  • Personal collections and hobbies
  • Achievement and award displays
  • Books of condolence for messages

Themed Celebrations

From military honours to sports team colours, hobby displays to cultural traditions - we help you create a ceremony that reflects their passions and beliefs.

  • Military and service honours
  • Sports and team tributes
  • Cultural and religious customs
  • Hobby and interest themes

Personal Touches

Small details that make a big difference - from favourite flowers to meaningful readings, every element can be chosen to reflect their unique personality.

  • Flowers from your loved one's garden
  • Personal tributes (poems, songs)
  • Special clothing or uniforms
  • Family members as pallbearers
  • Personalized chapel of rest arrangements
  • Meaningful symbols and keepsakes

Ways to Personalise

Memorial Stationery

Custom order of service books, prayer cards, and memorial bookmarks featuring their photograph and personal message.

Floral Tributes

Bespoke arrangements in their favourite colours, flowers that held special meaning, or designs that reflect their interests.
