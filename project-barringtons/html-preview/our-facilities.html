Our Facilities

Thoughtfully Designed Spaces

Creating an environment of peace and comfort

Our funeral homes have been carefully designed to provide a tranquil and dignified environment where families can gather, remember, and say goodbye. Every detail has been considered to ensure your comfort and privacy during this difficult time.

Chapel of Rest

Our peaceful chapel provides a serene setting for private family viewings and intimate services. The space is thoughtfully lit and decorated to create a calming atmosphere.

  • Comfortable seating for up to 30 people
  • Natural lighting with soft ambient options
  • Climate controlled environment
  • Private entrance and exit
  • Wheelchair accessible

Private Viewing Rooms

Quiet, comfortable rooms where families can spend private time with their loved one in a peaceful and dignified setting.

  • Intimate spaces for personal reflection
  • Comfortable seating and soft furnishings
  • Adjustable lighting for preference
  • Private access and complete privacy
  • Available 24 hours by appointment

Reception Areas

Welcoming spaces where families and friends can gather before and after services, providing comfort and support to one another.

  • Comfortable lounge seating
  • Tea and coffee facilities
  • Quiet conversation areas
  • Reading materials and memorial books
  • Children's corner with appropriate materials

Arrangement Rooms

Private, comfortable offices where our funeral directors meet with families to discuss arrangements in a confidential and supportive environment.

  • Private consultation spaces
  • Comfortable seating for families

  • Complete privacy and confidentiality
  • Accessible ground floor location

  • Highest standards of hygiene and care
