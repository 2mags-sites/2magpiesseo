Services Overview

Comprehensive Funeral Services

Every life is unique, and every farewell should be too. We offer a complete range of services to create a meaningful tribute that honors your loved one's memory.

Professional Standards: As proud members of NAFD, SAIF, Association of Green Funeral Directors, and recommended by the Good Funeral Guide, we maintain the highest standards in funeral care. Our director David Barrington served as NAFD President 2019-2021 and is a NAFD Approved Tutor.

Traditional Funerals

Classic, dignified services following time-honored traditions. We'll help you create a respectful farewell with all the traditional elements your family values.

  • Personalized chapel of rest viewing
  • Hearse and limousines
  • Traditional coffins
  • Formal funeral service
  • Family members as pallbearers
  • Books of condolence

Contemporary Celebrations

Modern celebrations of life that focus on joy and remembrance. Create a unique farewell that truly reflects the person you're honoring.

  • Personalized themes and decorations
  • Memory boards with life photos
  • Video tributes and slideshows
  • Personal tributes (poems, songs)
  • Travel past meaningful places
  • Flowers from your loved one's garden

Direct Cremation

A simple, affordable option without a traditional service. Perfect for those who prefer a private goodbye or plan to hold a memorial later.

  • Simple coffin included
  • Collection and care
  • All necessary paperwork
  • Return of ashes

Green Funerals

Environmentally conscious options for those who lived sustainably. We offer various eco-friendly choices to minimize environmental impact, including our memorial tree planting service.

  • Biodegradable coffins
  • Natural burial grounds
  • Memorial tree planted for every funeral
  • Electric hearse and limousine options
  • Carbon offset options

Religious Services

Respectful services honoring all faiths and beliefs. We work with religious leaders from all denominations to ensure traditions are observed.

  • Catholic services
  • Protestant services
  • Jewish ceremonies
  • Islamic traditions
  • Hindu rituals
  • Buddhist customs

Home Funerals

Intimate farewells in familiar surroundings. We can arrange for your loved one to rest at home before the funeral, allowing private family time.

  • Home chapel setup
  • Cooling equipment
  • 24/7 support
  • Flexible visiting
