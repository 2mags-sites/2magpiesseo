Common Questions

Can I change my mind?

Absolutely. Your plan can be updated anytime, and prepaid plans can be cancelled with a full refund if unused.

What if I move away?

Your plan can be transferred to any funeral director in the UK, or we can arrange services anywhere in the country.

Is my money safe?

Yes. All prepaid funds are held in an independent trust or insurance policy, completely separate from our business.

What's included in the price?

Our services, care of your loved one, coffin, hearse, and administration. Third-party costs like crematorium fees are also covered if you choose.

Can I plan for someone else?

Yes, with their consent. Many people arrange plans for elderly parents or relatives to ensure their wishes are met.

How do I start?

Simply call us on 0151 928 1625 or complete our online form. There's no pressure, just a friendly chat about your options.
