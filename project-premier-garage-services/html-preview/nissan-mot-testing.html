Common Nissan MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Nissan vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

CVT Transmission Problems

Nissan CVT transmissions, particularly in Qashqai and X-Trail models, require careful assessment. We check for proper operation, unusual vibrations, and fluid condition during MOT testing.

Electric Vehicle Systems

Nissan Leaf and e-NV200 require specialist EV knowledge. We safely test high-voltage systems, battery condition, and charging port functionality for MOT compliance.

ProPILOT & Safety Systems

Modern Nissan vehicles with ProPILOT assistance require proper testing of sensors, cameras, and safety systems to ensure they don't interfere with MOT requirements.

Emissions & DPF Issues

Nissan diesel models can suffer from DPF problems and emissions issues. We thoroughly test emissions compliance and check for proper DPF regeneration functionality.

Frequently Asked Questions - Nissan MOT Testing

How often does my Nissan need an MOT test?

Your Nissan requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Nissan Leaf or other electric model?

Yes, we're fully qualified to MOT test all Nissan electric vehicles, including the Leaf, e-NV200, and Ariya. We have specialist equipment and training for safe high-voltage system testing.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Nissan warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Nissan warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Nissan fails its MOT test?

If your Nissan fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Nissan parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Nissan parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
