Common Mazda MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Mazda vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

SkyActiv Technology

Mazda's SkyActiv engines and transmissions require specialist knowledge. We understand the unique characteristics of these systems and ensure proper operation during MOT testing.

i-stop System Issues

Mazda's i-stop start/stop technology can develop faults that affect MOT compliance. We check battery condition, starter motor function, and system operation thoroughly.

Corrosion Problems

Older Mazda models, particularly early MX-5s and Mazda6 models, can suffer from corrosion issues. We thoroughly inspect sills, subframes, and wheel arches for structural integrity.

Suspension Components

Mazda vehicles, especially CX series SUVs, can experience wear in suspension bushes and shock absorber mounts. We pay special attention to these safety-critical components.

Frequently Asked Questions - Mazda MOT Testing

How often does my Mazda need an MOT test?

Your Mazda requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test Mazda vehicles with SkyActiv technology?

Yes, our technicians are experienced with Mazda's SkyActiv engines and transmissions. We understand the unique characteristics of these systems and can properly assess them during MOT testing.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Mazda warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Mazda warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Mazda fails its MOT test?

If your Mazda fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Mazda parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Mazda parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
