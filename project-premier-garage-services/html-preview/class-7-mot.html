What Vehicles Need Class 7 MOT Testing?

Class 7 MOT testing is designed for commercial vehicles with a maximum authorised mass between 3,000kg and 3,500kg. These vehicles typically operate under greater stress and require enhanced safety standards due to their commercial use and loading capacity.

Vehicles requiring Class 7 MOT include:

  • Large panel vans (3000-3500kg)
  • Box vans and luton vans
  • Refrigerated delivery vehicles
  • Pickup trucks with commercial bodies
  • Small delivery trucks and lorries
  • Tipper trucks under 3500kg
  • Mobile workshop vehicles
  • Converted commercial vehicles

Enhanced Class 7 MOT Inspection Standards

Class 7 MOT testing includes all standard vehicle checks plus additional requirements specific to commercial vehicles and their loading capabilities. Our qualified testers follow strict DVSA guidelines tailored to the unique demands of commercial vehicle operation.

Additional Class 7 inspection areas include:

  • Enhanced brake testing for loaded vehicle weights
  • Loading area structural integrity and security
  • Tailgate and loading equipment functionality
  • Commercial vehicle lighting requirements
  • Tyre load rating and condition assessment
  • Suspension system capability under load
  • Rear vision and safety equipment
  • Load securing points and anchorages

These additional checks ensure your commercial vehicle can operate safely when fully loaded and meets all legal requirements for business use.

Common Class 7 MOT Considerations

Commercial vehicles face unique challenges due to their heavy-duty use, loading requirements, and business-critical operations. Our experience with Class 7 testing has identified these common areas requiring attention:

  • Brake systems: Enhanced performance requirements due to maximum loaded weight
  • Suspension wear: Heavy loading can accelerate wear on springs and shock absorbers
  • Tyre condition: Commercial use often leads to irregular wear patterns and damage
  • Loading equipment: Tailgates, ramps, and securing points must be safe and functional
  • Bodywork integrity: Loading damage and corrosion from commercial use
  • Lighting systems: Work lights and enhanced visibility requirements

Regular maintenance schedules are crucial for commercial vehicles to avoid unexpected failures and business disruption.

Class 7 MOT Requirements & Business Compliance

Preparing Your Commercial Vehicle

Class 7 vehicles require specific preparation to ensure efficient testing:

  • Ensure vehicle is completely unloaded for accurate weight testing
  • Clean loading area for structural inspection
  • Check all commercial lighting and warning systems
  • Verify tyre pressures are correct for unloaded weight
  • Bring vehicle documentation and weight certificates
  • Ensure access to all inspection points is clear

Business Considerations

For commercial operators, ensure you have appropriate operator licences, goods vehicle insurance, and driver qualification cards. Regular MOT testing is just one part of your compliance requirements for commercial vehicle operation.

Frequently Asked Questions About Class 7 MOT

What weight vehicles need Class 7 MOT testing?

Class 7 MOT is required for vehicles with a maximum authorised mass between 3,000kg and 3,500kg. This is the gross vehicle weight including maximum payload, not the unladen weight. Check your vehicle's weight plate for the correct figure.

How long does a Class 7 MOT take?

A Class 7 MOT typically takes 60-90 minutes due to the additional commercial vehicle checks required. This includes enhanced brake testing, loading area inspection, and commercial equipment verification.

Do I need to unload my van for the MOT?

Yes, your vehicle should be completely unloaded for the MOT test. This allows accurate weight measurement, proper brake testing, and full inspection of the loading area and suspension components.

Can you test refrigerated vans and specialist vehicles?

Yes, we can test refrigerated vans, tipper trucks, and other specialist commercial vehicles that fall under Class 7. We'll inspect any additional equipment such as tail lifts, refrigeration units, and hydraulic systems as part of the test.

Do you offer fleet MOT services?

Yes, we offer competitive rates for fleet vehicles and can schedule multiple Class 7 MOTs to minimise business disruption. We can also provide reminder services and maintenance planning for commercial vehicle fleets.

What if my commercial vehicle fails its MOT?
