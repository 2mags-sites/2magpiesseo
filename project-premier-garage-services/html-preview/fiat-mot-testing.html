Common Fiat MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Fiat vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

MultiJet Engine Issues

Fiat MultiJet diesel engines can experience DPF and EGR valve problems affecting emissions. We thoroughly test these systems to ensure MOT compliance and optimal performance.

Dualogic Transmission

Fiat's Dualogic automated manual transmission requires specific testing procedures. We understand these systems and ensure proper gear selection and clutch operation during MOT.

Electrical Systems

Modern Fiat models feature complex electrical systems including City steering and parking sensors. We test all safety-related electrical functions for MOT compliance.

Suspension & Steering

Fiat vehicles, particularly the 500 and Panda, can experience front suspension strut mount wear and steering rack issues. We inspect these components thoroughly.

Frequently Asked Questions - Fiat MOT Testing

How often does my Fiat need an MOT test?

Your Fiat requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Fiat 500e electric model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all Fiat electric vehicles, including the 500e and all hybrid variants. Electric vehicles undergo the same MOT test as conventional models, with appropriate safety procedures for electric systems.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Fiat warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Fiat warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Fiat fails its MOT test?

If your Fiat fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Fiat parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Fiat parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
