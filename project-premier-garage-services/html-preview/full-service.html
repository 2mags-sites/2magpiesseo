What's Included in Our Full Service

Exhaust & Emissions: Complete exhaust system inspection, emissions test, catalytic converter check, and exhaust mounting examination.

Full Service Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a full service different from other services?

Our full service is the most comprehensive maintenance package available, featuring a 67-point inspection compared to basic services that typically cover 15-20 points. It includes detailed examination of all major systems, road testing, and a comprehensive digital health report with photographic evidence.

How long does a full service take?

A thorough full service typically takes 3-4 hours to complete properly. This time allows our technicians to conduct all 67 inspection points, perform necessary maintenance, and complete road testing. We never rush the process to ensure nothing is missed.

Will I get a detailed report of what was checked?

Yes, you'll receive a comprehensive digital health check report showing all 67 inspection points, with colour-coded priority levels and photographic evidence of any issues. This helps you understand your vehicle's condition and plan future maintenance effectively.

Is a full service necessary for newer cars under warranty?

Absolutely. Regular full servicing is essential to maintain your manufacturer's warranty. Our service meets all manufacturer requirements and provides detailed documentation to support warranty claims. It also helps identify potential issues early, often while still covered under warranty.

Can I combine a full service with an MOT test?

Yes, we offer combined service and MOT packages that provide excellent value. Many of the checks overlap, making it convenient and cost-effective to have both done together. We can coordinate timing to ensure minimal disruption to your schedule.

What happens if you find problems during the service?

If we discover any issues, we'll contact you immediately with a detailed explanation and transparent quote for repairs. We prioritize issues by safety and urgency, and never proceed with additional work without your explicit approval. Many repairs can be completed the same day if required.
