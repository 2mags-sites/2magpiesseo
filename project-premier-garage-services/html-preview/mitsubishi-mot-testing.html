Common Mitsubishi MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Mitsubishi vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

PHEV Battery Systems

Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV requires specialist knowledge of plug-in hybrid systems. We safely test battery condition, charging systems, and high-voltage components during MOT testing.

4WD System Components

Mitsubishi's advanced 4WD systems, including Super Select 4WD, require expert assessment. We check transfer cases, differentials, and electronic control systems for proper operation.

Suspension & Chassis

L200 and Shogun models can develop wear in suspension components and chassis mounting points. We thoroughly inspect these heavy-duty vehicles for structural integrity.

Emissions Control

Mitsubishi diesel models require careful emissions testing. We ensure DPF systems and SCR technology function correctly to meet strict MOT emissions standards.

Frequently Asked Questions - Mitsubishi MOT Testing

How often does my Mitsubishi need an MOT test?

Your Mitsubishi requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV?

Yes, we're fully qualified to MOT test the Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV and other plug-in hybrid vehicles. We have specialist equipment and training for safe high-voltage system testing.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Mitsubishi warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Mitsubishi warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Mitsubishi fails its MOT test?

If your Mitsubishi fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Mitsubishi parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Mitsubishi parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
