Common Peugeot MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Peugeot vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

EAT8 Gearbox Issues

Peugeot's EAT8 automatic transmission can develop electronic faults affecting gear selection. We thoroughly test all transmission functions during MOT to ensure proper operation.

Exhaust Systems

Peugeot vehicles are prone to exhaust system corrosion, particularly around the rear silencer and flex pipe connections. We inspect all exhaust components thoroughly.

Suspension Components

Front suspension top mounts and rear axle bushes are common wear points on Peugeot models. We pay special attention to these areas during inspection.

Electronic Systems

Modern Peugeots feature complex electronic systems including touchscreen controls and i-Cockpit displays. We test all safety-related electronic functions for MOT compliance.

Frequently Asked Questions - Peugeot MOT Testing

How often does my Peugeot need an MOT test?

Your Peugeot requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Peugeot e-208 or e-2008 electric model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all Peugeot electric vehicles, including the e-208, e-2008, and all hybrid variants. These vehicles undergo the same MOT test as conventional models.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Peugeot warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Peugeot warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Peugeot fails its MOT test?

If your Peugeot fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Peugeot parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Peugeot parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
