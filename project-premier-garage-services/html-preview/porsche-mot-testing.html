Common Porsche MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Porsche vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

Ceramic Brake Systems

Porsche's PCCB ceramic brake systems require specialist knowledge for proper assessment. We understand the unique characteristics and testing requirements of these high-performance braking systems.

PDLS Lighting Systems

Porsche Dynamic Light System (PDLS) features complex adaptive lighting. We test all PDLS functions, cornering lights, and ensure proper beam patterns meet MOT requirements.

PASM Suspension

Porsche Active Suspension Management requires expert testing. We check all suspension modes, damper operation, and ensure electronic systems function correctly for MOT compliance.

Emissions & Performance

High-performance Porsche engines require precise emissions testing. We ensure your vehicle meets strict Euro emissions standards while maintaining optimal performance characteristics.

Frequently Asked Questions - Porsche MOT Testing

How often does my Porsche need an MOT test?

Your Porsche requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Porsche Taycan electric vehicle?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test the Porsche Taycan and all electric Porsche models. Electric vehicles undergo the same MOT test as conventional models, with special attention to high-voltage system safety.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Porsche warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Porsche warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Porsche fails its MOT test?

If your Porsche fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Porsche parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Porsche parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
