Common Suzuki MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Suzuki vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

Mild Hybrid Systems

Suzuki's mild hybrid technology in Swift and Ignis models requires specialist knowledge. We check the 12V lithium-ion battery, starter generator, and regenerative braking systems.

ALLGRIP 4WD Systems

Suzuki's ALLGRIP four-wheel drive system in Vitara and S-Cross requires expert assessment. We test electronic coupling, differential locks, and traction control integration.

Corrosion Issues

Older Suzuki models, particularly Jimny and Grand Vitara, can suffer from corrosion in chassis components. We thoroughly inspect structural areas for MOT compliance.

Small Engine Components

Suzuki's compact engines require careful emissions testing. We ensure proper function of catalytic converters, lambda sensors, and evaporative emission systems.

Frequently Asked Questions - Suzuki MOT Testing

How often does my Suzuki need an MOT test?

Your Suzuki requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test Suzuki vehicles with mild hybrid systems?

Yes, we're experienced with Suzuki's mild hybrid technology found in models like the Swift and Ignis. We understand how to properly test these systems during the MOT process.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Suzuki warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Suzuki warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Suzuki fails its MOT test?

If your Suzuki fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Suzuki parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Suzuki parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
