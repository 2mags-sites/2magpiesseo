Proper wheel balancing is essential for vehicle safety, comfort, and tyre longevity. Our precision balancing service eliminates vibrations and ensures even tyre wear, maximizing your investment in new tyres.

Frequently Asked Questions About Tyre Fitting

How long does professional tyre fitting take?

Professional tyre fitting typically takes 30-45 minutes for a set of four tyres, including wheel balancing. Single tyre fitting usually takes 15-20 minutes. We prioritize quality over speed to ensure perfect results every time.

Do you fit tyres purchased elsewhere?

Yes, we're happy to fit tyres purchased elsewhere. We charge a competitive fitting fee that includes wheel balancing, valve check, and proper disposal of old tyres. We cannot warranty tyres we didn't supply, but we guarantee our fitting workmanship.

Is wheel balancing really necessary with new tyres?

Absolutely. Even new tyres and wheels need balancing due to manufacturing tolerances. Proper balancing eliminates vibration, ensures even wear, improves fuel economy, and protects your vehicle's suspension components. We include it as standard.

Can you handle TPMS sensors during fitting?

Yes, we have the equipment and expertise to handle Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) sensors. We can transfer existing sensors to new tyres and reprogram the system as required. Additional charges may apply for TPMS services.

What happens to my old tyres after fitting?

We dispose of old tyres responsibly through licensed waste carriers in accordance with environmental regulations. The disposal cost is included in our fitting service, and tyres are recycled or processed for other uses rather than going to landfill.

Do you offer mobile tyre fitting services?

While we primarily operate from our fully equipped workshop for the best quality and safety, we do offer emergency roadside assistance for punctures and tyre emergencies. For full fitting services, we recommend visiting our garage for optimal results.
