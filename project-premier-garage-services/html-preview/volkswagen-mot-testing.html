Common Volkswagen MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Volkswagen vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

DSG Transmission Systems

VW's Direct Shift Gearbox (DSG) requires regular servicing and can affect vehicle safety systems. We check DSG operation, mechatronic unit function, and ensure proper gear selection during MOT testing.

TSI & TDI Engine Issues

Volkswagen's turbocharged engines can develop carbon build-up and timing chain issues. We thoroughly check engine performance, emissions output, and turbocharger operation during the MOT test.

Electronic Parking Brake

Many VW models feature electronic parking brakes that require specific testing procedures. We ensure EPB function, cable tension, and electronic control systems meet MOT safety requirements.

AdBlue & DPF Systems

VW diesel models use sophisticated emissions control including AdBlue injection and diesel particulate filters. We test these systems thoroughly to ensure MOT emissions compliance.

Frequently Asked Questions - Volkswagen MOT Testing

How often does my Volkswagen need an MOT test?

Your Volkswagen requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my VW ID electric or hybrid model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all Volkswagen electric vehicles (ID.3, ID.4) and plug-in hybrid models (Golf GTE, Passat GTE). We have the specialized equipment to safely test high-voltage systems.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Volkswagen warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Volkswagen warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Volkswagen fails its MOT test?

If your Volkswagen fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine VW parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Volkswagen parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice based on your budget.
