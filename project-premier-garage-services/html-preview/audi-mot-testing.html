Common Audi MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Audi vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

Suspension & Steering

Audi vehicles, particularly Q-series models, can experience suspension bush wear and anti-roll bar link issues. We pay special attention to these components during MOT testing.

Lighting Systems

Modern Audis feature complex LED and Matrix LED systems. We test all adaptive lighting functions, DRL systems, and ensure proper beam alignment for MOT compliance.

Emissions & DPF

Diesel Audi models require careful emissions testing. We ensure your DPF system is functioning correctly and emissions meet the strict Euro standards required for MOT pass.

Brake Systems

Audi's sophisticated brake systems, including those with ceramic discs on RS models, require expert assessment. We check brake efficiency, disc condition, and pad wear thoroughly.

Frequently Asked Questions - Audi MOT Testing

How often does my Audi need an MOT test?

Your Audi requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Audi e-tron or hybrid model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all Audi electric and hybrid vehicles, including the e-tron, e-tron GT, and all plug-in hybrid variants. These vehicles undergo the same MOT test as conventional models.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Audi warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Audi warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Audi fails its MOT test?

If your Audi fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Audi parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Audi parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
