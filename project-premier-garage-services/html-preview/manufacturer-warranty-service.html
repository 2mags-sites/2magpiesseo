Service Schedules & Requirements

Common Service Intervals

Most manufacturers require servicing at specific intervals to maintain warranty coverage:

  • Annual service or 10,000-12,000 miles (whichever comes first)
  • Major service every 20,000-24,000 miles or 2 years
  • Timing belt replacement as per manufacturer schedule
  • Brake fluid change every 2-3 years

What to Bring

Frequently Asked Questions About Manufacturer Warranty Service

Will independent servicing affect my manufacturer warranty?

No, absolutely not. Under EU Block Exemption Regulations, you have the legal right to have your vehicle serviced at any qualified garage without affecting your manufacturer warranty, provided the service meets manufacturer standards. We ensure complete compliance with all requirements.

Do you use genuine parts for warranty services?

Yes, we use only genuine manufacturer parts or approved OEM equivalents that meet or exceed manufacturer specifications. This ensures your warranty remains valid while often providing better value than main dealer pricing.

How much can I save compared to main dealer servicing?

Our customers typically save 30-40% compared to main dealer prices while receiving exactly the same quality of service and parts. We provide transparent pricing with detailed quotes before any work begins, so you'll know exactly what you're paying for.

How do you document the service for warranty purposes?

We provide complete documentation including official service book stamping, digital service records, parts certificates, and detailed work reports. This documentation fully satisfies manufacturer warranty requirements and maintains your vehicle's resale value.

Can you service all vehicle makes and models?

We service all major vehicle makes including Ford, Vauxhall, BMW, Audi, Mercedes, Volkswagen, Toyota, Honda, and many others. Our technicians are trained on multiple manufacturer systems and we have access to manufacturer-specific diagnostic equipment and service information.

What happens if warranty work is needed after service?
