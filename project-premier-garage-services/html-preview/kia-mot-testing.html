Common Kia MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Kia vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

Brake System Components

Kia vehicles can experience brake disc corrosion and pad wear, particularly on rear axles. We thoroughly inspect brake efficiency, disc condition, and the electronic parking brake systems found on newer models.

Suspension & Steering

Common issues include front suspension strut top mounts and rear suspension bushes. We pay special attention to the electronic power steering systems and suspension components during MOT testing.

Emissions & Engine

Kia diesel models require careful emissions testing, including DPF functionality. We ensure your engine management systems are working correctly and emissions meet the required Euro standards.

Electrical Systems

Modern Kia vehicles feature sophisticated safety systems including ESC, ABS, and advanced lighting. We test all electronic safety systems to ensure MOT compliance while respecting warranty considerations.

Frequently Asked Questions - Kia MOT Testing

How often does my Kia need an MOT test?

Your Kia requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Kia 7-year warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Kia warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station without affecting warranty coverage.

Can you test my Kia hybrid or electric model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all Kia hybrid and electric vehicles, including the Niro, EV6, and e-Niro models. These vehicles undergo the same MOT test as conventional models.

What if my Kia fails its MOT test?

If your Kia fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Kia parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Kia parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. For vehicles under warranty, we'll discuss the best options to maintain your warranty coverage while providing transparent pricing.
