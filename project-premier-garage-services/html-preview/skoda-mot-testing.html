Frequently Asked Questions - Skoda MOT Testing

How often does my Skoda need an MOT test?

Your Skoda requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Skoda Enyaq iV electric model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all Skoda electric vehicles, including the Enyaq iV and all hybrid variants. Electric vehicles undergo the same MOT test as conventional models, with appropriate safety procedures.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Skoda warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Skoda warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Skoda fails its MOT test?

If your Skoda fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Skoda parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Skoda parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
