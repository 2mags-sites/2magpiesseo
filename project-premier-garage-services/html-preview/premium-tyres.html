Tyre Selection & Maintenance

Choosing the Right Premium Tyres

Selecting the correct premium tyres depends on several factors:

  • Vehicle Type: Car, SUV, van, or high-performance vehicle requirements
  • Driving Style: Comfort-focused, sporty, or high-performance driving
  • Climate: Summer, winter, or all-season tyre requirements
  • Usage: City driving, motorway, or mixed driving conditions

Premium Tyre Maintenance

Maximize your premium tyre investment with proper maintenance:

  • Check tyre pressure monthly using manufacturer specifications
  • Regular rotation every 6,000-8,000 miles for even wear
  • Professional wheel alignment checks annually
  • Visual inspection for damage, cuts, or uneven wear patterns

We provide free tyre pressure checks and advice on maximizing the life and performance of your premium tyres.

Frequently Asked Questions About Premium Tyres

What makes premium tyres worth the extra cost?

Premium tyres offer superior safety with shorter braking distances, better wet grip, improved fuel economy, longer lifespan, enhanced comfort, and reduced road noise. The initial investment typically pays for itself through longer life, fuel savings, and enhanced safety.

How long do premium tyres typically last?

Premium tyres typically last 20,000-40,000 miles depending on driving style, vehicle type, and maintenance. They generally last 20-40% longer than budget tyres due to advanced compounds and construction, providing better value over time.

Can I mix premium tyres with my existing tyres?

While possible, we recommend replacing tyres in pairs (both front or both rear) for optimal performance and safety. Mixing different tyre brands or types can affect handling and stability. We'll advise the best approach for your specific situation.
