Common Mercedes MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Mercedes vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

Air Suspension Systems

Many Mercedes models feature AIRMATIC air suspension that can develop leaks or compressor issues. We thoroughly test air suspension operation, height sensors, and ride level control during MOT testing.

MBUX & Electronic Systems

Mercedes' advanced MBUX infotainment system controls many vehicle functions. We ensure all safety-related electronic systems are functioning correctly, including ESP, ABS, and parking assist systems.

4MATIC All-Wheel Drive

Mercedes 4MATIC system components require specific attention during MOT testing, including transfer case operation, driveshaft joints, and differential systems for proper safety compliance.

AdBlue & Emissions

Mercedes diesel models use AdBlue SCR technology for emissions control. We test NOx sensor function, AdBlue quality, and diesel particulate filter operation to ensure MOT emissions compliance.

Frequently Asked Questions - Mercedes MOT Testing

How often does my Mercedes need an MOT test?

Your Mercedes requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Mercedes EQC or hybrid model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all Mercedes electric vehicles (EQC, EQA, EQS) and plug-in hybrid models. We have the specialized equipment to safely test high-voltage systems and electric drivetrains.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Mercedes warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Mercedes warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Mercedes fails its MOT test?

If your Mercedes fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Mercedes parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Mercedes-Benz parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice based on your requirements.
