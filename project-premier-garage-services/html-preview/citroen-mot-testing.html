Common Citroen MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Citroen vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

Hydropneumatic Suspension

Classic Citroen models with hydropneumatic suspension require specialist knowledge for MOT testing. We understand these unique systems and test them properly for safety compliance.

Electrical Systems

Citroen vehicles can experience electrical issues affecting lights, indicators, and dashboard warnings. We thoroughly test all electrical systems during MOT inspection.

Exhaust & Emissions

Diesel particulate filters and catalytic converters are common failure points on Citroen models. We ensure your emissions system meets strict MOT requirements.

Brake Systems

Brake disc corrosion and pad wear are frequent issues on Citroen vehicles. We check brake efficiency, disc condition, and hydraulic systems thoroughly during testing.

Frequently Asked Questions - Citroen MOT Testing

How often does my Citroen need an MOT test?

Your Citroen requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test older Citroen models with hydropneumatic suspension?

Yes, we have the specialist knowledge and equipment to properly test Citroen vehicles with hydropneumatic suspension systems. Our technicians understand these unique systems and test them according to MOT requirements.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Citroen warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Citroen warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Citroen fails its MOT test?

If your Citroen fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Citroen parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Citroen parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
