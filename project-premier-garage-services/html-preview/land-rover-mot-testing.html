Common Land Rover MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Land Rover vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

Air Suspension Systems

Land Rover's air suspension systems are complex and can develop leaks or compressor issues. We thoroughly test all air suspension components and check for proper ride height during MOT testing.

Terrain Response Systems

Advanced terrain management systems can trigger warning lights that affect MOT compliance. We diagnose all electronic systems to ensure they're functioning correctly for your test.

Adaptive Lighting

Modern Land Rovers feature sophisticated adaptive LED lighting systems. We verify all lighting modes work correctly and headlight alignment meets strict MOT requirements.

Electronic Handbrake

Electronic parking brake systems require specialist testing procedures. We ensure proper operation and check that all related warning systems function correctly for MOT compliance.

Frequently Asked Questions - Land Rover MOT Testing

How often does my Land Rover need an MOT test?

Your Land Rover requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you handle my Land Rover's air suspension during MOT?

Yes, we have specialist equipment and knowledge to properly test Land Rover air suspension systems. We check for leaks, proper operation, and correct ride height as required for MOT compliance.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Land Rover warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Land Rover warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Land Rover fails its MOT test?

If your Land Rover fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Land Rover parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Land Rover parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
