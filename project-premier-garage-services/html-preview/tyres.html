Tyre Safety & Maintenance

Proper tyre maintenance is crucial for safety, performance, and longevity. We provide expert advice on maintaining your tyres and help you understand when replacement is necessary.

Key safety considerations:

  • Tread Depth: Legal minimum is 1.6mm, but 3mm recommended for safety
  • Tyre Pressure: Check monthly and before long journeys
  • Visual Inspection: Look for cuts, bulges, uneven wear, or embedded objects
  • Age Considerations: Tyres over 6 years old should be inspected annually
  • Rotation Service: Regular rotation promotes even wear and extends life
  • Alignment Check: Proper alignment prevents premature wear

We offer free tyre safety checks and can advise on the most cost-effective tyre replacement strategy for your vehicle.

How long does tyre fitting take?

Standard tyre fitting typically takes 30-45 minutes per tyre, including removal, fitting, balancing, and pressure setting. A complete set of four tyres usually takes 2-3 hours. We can often complete the work while you wait.

Can you repair punctures in all tyres?

We can repair most punctures in the central tread area of the tyre, provided the damage is within safety guidelines. Punctures in the sidewall, shoulder area, or those larger than 6mm diameter typically require tyre replacement for safety reasons.

Do you offer mobile tyre fitting?

Yes, we provide mobile tyre fitting services within the local Stockport area for emergencies and convenience. This service is subject to availability and includes the same professional fitting and balancing as our workshop service.

When should I replace my tyres?

Replace tyres when tread depth reaches 1.6mm (legal minimum) or 3mm for optimal safety. Also replace if you notice uneven wear, sidewall damage, bulges, or if tyres are over 6 years old. We provide free tyre safety checks to assess condition.

Do you stock tyres for all vehicles?

We stock a comprehensive range of tyres for cars, vans, and light commercial vehicles. If we don't have your specific size in stock, we can usually source and fit them within 24 hours. We also specialise in run-flat and low-profile tyres.
