Common Honda MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Honda vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

CVT Transmission Issues

Honda CVT transmissions require specific checks during MOT testing. We inspect for proper operation, unusual noises, and fluid condition that could affect vehicle performance and safety.

i-VTEC Engine Systems

Honda's i-VTEC engines have unique characteristics requiring specialist knowledge. We check the VTEC operation, oil pressure systems, and ensure proper emissions compliance.

Suspension Components

Honda vehicles, particularly the CR-V and Pilot, can develop wear in suspension bushes and shock absorber mounts. We thoroughly inspect these safety-critical components.

Brake System Efficiency

Honda's brake systems, including those with brake assist and ABS, require precise testing. We ensure brake efficiency meets MOT standards and all safety systems function correctly.

Frequently Asked Questions - Honda MOT Testing

How often does my Honda need an MOT test?

Your Honda requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test Honda vehicles with CVT transmissions?

Yes, we have specialist knowledge of Honda CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission) systems. We can properly assess these transmissions during the MOT test and identify any issues that may affect safety or performance.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Honda warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Honda warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Honda fails its MOT test?

If your Honda fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Honda parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Honda parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
