Common BMW MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing BMW vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

iDrive & Electronic Systems

BMW's advanced iDrive system controls many vehicle functions. We check that all safety-related electronic systems are functioning correctly for MOT compliance, including parking sensors and stability control.

xDrive System Components

BMW's all-wheel-drive xDrive system has specific components that require attention during MOT testing, including transfer case mounts, driveshaft joints, and differential systems.

Suspension & Run-Flat Tyres

Many BMWs use run-flat tyres and sophisticated suspension systems. We carefully inspect suspension components, tyre condition, and pressure monitoring systems during the MOT test.

Diesel Particulate Filters

BMW diesel models require careful DPF and emissions testing. We ensure your AdBlue system (where fitted) and diesel particulate filter are functioning correctly to meet MOT emissions standards.

Frequently Asked Questions - BMW MOT Testing

How often does my BMW need an MOT test?

Your BMW requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my BMW electric or hybrid model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all BMW electric vehicles (i3, i4, iX) and plug-in hybrid models. We have the specialized equipment to safely test high-voltage systems and electric drivetrains.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my BMW warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your BMW warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my BMW fails its MOT test?

If your BMW fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine BMW parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine BMW parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice based on your budget and preferences.
