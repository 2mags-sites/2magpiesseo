Common Jeep MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Jeep vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

4x4 & Transfer Case Systems

Jeep's sophisticated 4WD systems including Selec-Trac and Rock-Trac require specialist knowledge. We test transfer case operation, differential locks, and ensure all drivetrain components meet MOT requirements.

Suspension & Steering

Jeep vehicles, particularly Wrangler models, can experience suspension bushing wear and steering damper issues. We thoroughly inspect the solid axle setups and independent suspension systems.

Emissions & Engine

Jeep engines, including the Pentastar V6 and EcoDiesel variants, require careful emissions testing. We ensure catalytic converters, EGR systems, and DPF components meet Euro standards.

Electrical & Safety Systems

Modern Jeep vehicles feature advanced safety systems including hill descent control, electronic stability control, and traction management. We test all electronic safety systems thoroughly.

Frequently Asked Questions - Jeep MOT Testing

How often does my Jeep need an MOT test?

Your Jeep requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Jeep's 4x4 system during MOT?

Yes, our MOT testing includes assessment of 4WD systems where they affect safety. We can test transfer case operation, differential locks, and electronic 4x4 systems to ensure they meet MOT requirements.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Jeep warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Jeep warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Jeep fails its MOT test?

If your Jeep fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Jeep parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Mopar parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
