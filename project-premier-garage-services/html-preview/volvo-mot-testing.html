Common Volvo MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Volvo vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

Advanced Safety Systems

Volvo's comprehensive IntelliSafe suite includes multiple sensors and cameras. We ensure all safety systems are functioning correctly and not displaying fault codes that could affect MOT compliance.

Four-C Suspension

Volvo's advanced Four-C (Continuously Controlled Chassis Concept) suspension requires specialist knowledge. We test all suspension modes and electronic damper controls thoroughly.

Sensus Infotainment

Modern Volvo Sensus systems integrate with safety functions that can trigger warning lights. We ensure all systems communicate correctly and display no MOT-affecting error codes.

Thor's Hammer Lighting

Volvo's distinctive LED lighting systems with automatic functions require expert testing. We verify all lighting modes operate correctly and beam patterns meet strict MOT requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions - Volvo MOT Testing

How often does my Volvo need an MOT test?

Your Volvo requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Volvo Recharge hybrid or electric model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all Volvo Recharge hybrid and electric models. We follow strict high-voltage safety procedures and have the specialist equipment needed for electric vehicle testing.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Volvo warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Volvo warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Volvo fails its MOT test?

If your Volvo fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Volvo parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Volvo parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
