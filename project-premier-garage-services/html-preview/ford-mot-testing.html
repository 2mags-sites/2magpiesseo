Common Ford MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Ford vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

EcoBoost Engine Issues

Ford's EcoBoost engines can develop coolant system problems and turbocharger issues. We thoroughly check cooling systems, intercoolers, and boost pressure during MOT testing.

PowerShift Transmission

Ford's dual-clutch PowerShift gearboxes require special attention for clutch wear and transmission fluid levels, which can affect vehicle safety systems tested during MOT.

Rear Beam Suspension

Many Ford models use torsion beam rear suspension that can suffer from bush wear and corrosion. We pay particular attention to these components during the MOT inspection.

Emissions & DPF Systems

Ford diesel models, particularly Transit and larger engines, can experience DPF blockages. We ensure your emissions system meets MOT standards and DPF is functioning correctly.

Frequently Asked Questions - Ford MOT Testing

How often does my Ford need an MOT test?

Your Ford requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Ford hybrid or EcoBoost model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all Ford hybrid vehicles and EcoBoost engines. We have the diagnostic equipment to properly test these advanced powertrains and their associated systems.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Ford warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Ford warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Ford fails its MOT test?

If your Ford fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Ford parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Ford parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
