Common Jaguar MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Jaguar vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

InControl Systems

Modern Jaguars feature complex InControl infotainment systems that can affect dashboard warning lights. We ensure all system diagnostics are clear for MOT compliance.

Adaptive Suspension

Jaguar's adaptive dynamics and air suspension systems require specialist knowledge. We thoroughly test suspension response and check for any fault codes that could cause MOT failure.

LED Matrix Lighting

Jaguar's advanced LED matrix headlights and adaptive lighting systems need expert testing. We verify all lighting functions work correctly and beam patterns meet MOT requirements.

Brake & Stability Systems

Jaguar's sophisticated brake systems include advanced stability and traction control. We test brake efficiency and ensure all electronic safety systems function correctly.

Frequently Asked Questions - Jaguar MOT Testing

How often does my Jaguar need an MOT test?

Your Jaguar requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Jaguar I-Pace electric vehicle?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test the Jaguar I-Pace and any future electric Jaguar models. Electric vehicles undergo the same MOT test as conventional models, with special attention to high-voltage systems safety.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Jaguar warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Jaguar warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Jaguar fails its MOT test?

If your Jaguar fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Jaguar parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Jaguar parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
