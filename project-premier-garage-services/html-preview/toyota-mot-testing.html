Common Toyota MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Toyota vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

Hybrid Battery Systems

Toyota hybrid models like the Prius require specialist knowledge. We check hybrid battery condition, cooling systems, and high-voltage safety components during MOT testing.

Corrosion Issues

Older Toyota models can suffer from rear subframe corrosion and sill deterioration. We thoroughly inspect these areas to ensure structural integrity meets MOT standards.

Brake Systems

Toyota's regenerative braking systems on hybrid models require expert assessment. We test brake efficiency and check for proper integration between electric and hydraulic systems.

Emissions Control

Toyota diesel models require careful emissions testing. We ensure your DPF system and catalytic converters are functioning correctly to meet strict Euro emissions standards.

Frequently Asked Questions - Toyota MOT Testing

How often does my Toyota need an MOT test?

Your Toyota requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Toyota Prius or other hybrid model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all Toyota hybrid vehicles, including the Prius, Corolla Hybrid, and RAV4 Hybrid. We have specialist equipment to safely test high-voltage systems.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Toyota warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Toyota warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Toyota fails its MOT test?

If your Toyota fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Toyota parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Toyota parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
