Common Tesla MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Tesla vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

Software Update Status

Tesla vehicles receive frequent over-the-air updates that can affect various systems. We ensure all safety-critical software is up to date and no pending updates are affecting MOT compliance systems.

Autopilot & FSD Systems

Advanced driver assistance systems require careful evaluation. We test that all camera sensors, radar systems, and warning functions operate correctly without displaying fault codes.

Regenerative Braking

Tesla's aggressive regenerative braking system requires specialist knowledge for proper brake efficiency testing. We ensure both regenerative and friction braking systems meet MOT requirements.

LED Matrix Lighting

Tesla's advanced LED lighting systems with automatic functions need expert testing. We verify all lighting modes work correctly and beam patterns meet strict MOT alignment requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions - Tesla MOT Testing

How often does my Tesla need an MOT test?

Your Tesla requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Do software updates affect my Tesla's MOT test?

Tesla's over-the-air updates can occasionally affect systems that are checked during MOT testing. We ensure your Tesla has completed any pending updates and all safety systems are functioning correctly before testing.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Tesla warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Tesla warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Tesla fails its MOT test?

If your Tesla fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Can you service my Tesla after the MOT?

While Tesla vehicles require minimal traditional servicing, we can perform brake fluid changes, cabin air filter replacements, and other maintenance items. We use quality parts and follow Tesla's service guidelines.
