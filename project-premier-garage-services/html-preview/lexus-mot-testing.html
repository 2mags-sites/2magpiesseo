Common Lexus MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Lexus vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

Hybrid Battery Systems

Lexus hybrid systems require specialist knowledge for proper MOT testing. We check high-voltage battery health, cooling systems, and ensure all hybrid warning lights are functioning correctly.

Regenerative Braking

Lexus vehicles use regenerative braking systems that require expert testing procedures. We ensure both electric and conventional braking systems meet MOT efficiency requirements.

Adaptive LED Systems

Lexus features advanced LED lighting with adaptive functionality. We test all lighting modes, automatic high beam, and ensure proper beam patterns meet strict MOT requirements.

Safety System Integration

Lexus Safety System+ includes multiple electronic aids that can affect MOT compliance. We ensure all safety systems are functioning correctly and not displaying warning lights.

Frequently Asked Questions - Lexus MOT Testing

How often does my Lexus need an MOT test?

Your Lexus requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you safely test my Lexus hybrid vehicle?

Yes, our technicians are trained in high-voltage safety procedures for hybrid vehicles. We follow strict safety protocols when testing Lexus hybrid systems and have specialist equipment for hybrid MOT testing.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Lexus warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Lexus warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Lexus fails its MOT test?

If your Lexus fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Lexus parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Lexus parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
