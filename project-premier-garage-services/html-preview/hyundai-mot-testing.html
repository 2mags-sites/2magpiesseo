Common Hyundai MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Hyundai vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

Mild Hybrid Systems

Modern Hyundai vehicles feature mild hybrid technology with 48V systems. We thoroughly test the electrical components, battery systems, and ensure all hybrid-related safety systems are functioning correctly.

Suspension Components

Hyundai vehicles can experience front suspension strut mount wear and rear shock absorber issues. We pay particular attention to the suspension bushes and anti-roll bar components during testing.

Emissions & Engine

Hyundai diesel models require careful emissions testing, particularly DPF functionality. We ensure your GDI petrol engines and diesel particulate filters meet the strict Euro emission standards.

Electronic Safety Systems

Advanced driver assistance systems including ESC, ABS, and collision avoidance require thorough testing. We ensure all Hyundai SmartSense safety features are operating within MOT parameters.

Frequently Asked Questions - Hyundai MOT Testing

How often does my Hyundai need an MOT test?

Your Hyundai requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Hyundai hybrid or electric model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all Hyundai hybrid, mild hybrid, and electric vehicles, including IONIQ models and mild hybrid systems. These vehicles undergo the same MOT test as conventional models.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Hyundai warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Hyundai warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Hyundai fails its MOT test?

If your Hyundai fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Hyundai parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Hyundai parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice.
