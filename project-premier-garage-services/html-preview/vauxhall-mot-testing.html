Common Vauxhall MOT Failure Points

Through years of testing Vauxhall vehicles, we've identified common areas that require attention:

EGR Valve & Emissions

Vauxhall diesel engines, particularly in Astra and Insignia models, can suffer from EGR valve carbon build-up affecting emissions. We thoroughly test exhaust gas recirculation systems during MOT testing.

Timing Chain Issues

Some Vauxhall engines, especially 1.0 and 1.4 turbo units, can develop timing chain stretch. We check for timing chain noise and proper engine timing that could affect MOT emission tests.

Electronic Handbrake

Many modern Vauxhall models feature electronic parking brakes that require specific testing procedures. We ensure EPB operation, force distribution, and electronic control systems meet safety standards.

Suspension & Bushes

Vauxhall vehicles commonly experience suspension bush wear, particularly on front anti-roll bar links and rear beam bushes. We pay close attention to these wear points during MOT inspection.

Frequently Asked Questions - Vauxhall MOT Testing

How often does my Vauxhall need an MOT test?

Your Vauxhall requires its first MOT test when it reaches 3 years old from the date of first registration. After that, it needs an annual MOT test to remain road legal. We'll send you reminders when your MOT is due.

Can you test my Vauxhall Corsa-e or electric model?

Yes, we're fully equipped and trained to MOT test all Vauxhall electric vehicles including the Corsa-e and Mokka-e. We have the specialized equipment to safely test high-voltage systems and electric motor functions.

Will an MOT at your garage affect my Vauxhall warranty?

No, having your MOT test performed at our RAC-approved garage will not affect your Vauxhall warranty. Under EU Block Exemption regulations, you can have your MOT done at any approved testing station.

What if my Vauxhall fails its MOT test?

If your Vauxhall fails its MOT, we'll provide a clear explanation of what needs fixing with a competitive quote for repairs. Once repairs are completed, we offer a free retest within 10 working days.

Do you use genuine Vauxhall parts for MOT repairs?

We offer both genuine Vauxhall parts and high-quality OEM alternatives. We'll always discuss options with you, providing transparent pricing for both so you can make an informed choice that suits your budget.
